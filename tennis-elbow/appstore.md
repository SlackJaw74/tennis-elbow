## Description
Tennis Elbow Recovery is your comprehensive companion for managing and recovering from lateral epicondylitis (tennis elbow). Whether you're an athlete, weekend warrior, or dealing with repetitive strain, this app provides structured treatment plans designed to help you heal.

**Key Features:**

• **Structured Treatment Plans** - Follow progressive weekly plans with exercises, stretches, and therapies tailored to different recovery stages

• **Activity Scheduling** - Schedule treatments for morning, afternoon, or evening across your week with smart reminders

• **Progress Tracking** - Monitor your pain levels, track exercise completion, and visualize your recovery journey with detailed charts

• **Exercise Library** - Access a comprehensive collection of exercises with step-by-step instructions, including:
  - Wrist extension and flexion stretches
  - Eccentric strengthening exercises
  - Forearm muscle conditioning
  - Ice therapy protocols

• **Pain Tracking** - Log and monitor your pain levels to understand what helps and adjust your treatment accordingly

• **Weight Progression** - Track exercise weights and gradually increase resistance as you get stronger

• **Customizable Activities** - Add notes, set difficulty levels, and personalize your treatment to match your needs

• **Complete Privacy** - All data is stored locally on your device. No cloud sync, no data sharing, no accounts required

**Perfect For:**
- Tennis players recovering from lateral epicondylitis
- Anyone experiencing elbow pain from repetitive activities
- People following physical therapy recommendations
- Those seeking to prevent tennis elbow recurrence

**Medical Disclaimer:**
This app is designed for educational purposes and activity tracking. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with a qualified healthcare professional before starting any treatment program.

Start your recovery with confidence. Download Tennis Elbow Recovery today and take control of your healing journey.
